Please welcome Pizza Possum!

A treasured resident of Kirkland area Pizza Possum is Alena's neighbor.

He enriches Kirkland life with his following favorite activites

  • Raising well behaved little ones
  • Watching over you while you sleep
  • Fending off troublesome raccoons
  • Returning lost toys to children's sandboxes
  • Helping maintain local plum trees
  • Stealing pizza from trash cans

How to befriend your neighborhood Possum

  • Don't finish all the pizza, be a friend and share
  • Always, always leave your trash can ajar
  • Plant as many plum trees as often as possible
  • Don't scream at them
  • Don't throw pennies and dollars
